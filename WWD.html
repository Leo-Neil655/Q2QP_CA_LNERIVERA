Initiatives
Our main initiative is to give more platform to push for a more green style of architecture in the Philippines. Green architecture involves a whole-building approach to sustainability by recognizing performance in human and environmental health. These involve sustainable site selection and development, energy efficiency, water conservation, clean air, use of low-impact materials, renewable resource, and preserving indoor air quality.
Involvements
Green AP has been involved with multiple calls to action and programs that exist in the Philippines. the organization has been active in sending members to take talks or discussions and show of concept to numeral attempts to gain traction for a more green architecture focus in the Philippines, working with businesses and institutions, all to fulfill the wish for a better utilization of green architecture in the country.
Activities/ Partipications
Some of our recent activities include WORLDBEX, Earth Day 2023, talks regarding Keppel Land, a roundtable discussion over sustainabilityin Christmas buffet, our 20th green forum, and much more. More events are set to come and be explored.
