"The Philippines' oldest multidisciplinary Green Architecture Organization"

Organization background
The Green Architecture Advocacy philippines is the oldest multidisciplinary Green Architecture organization. Having been formed for more than a decade by this point, the organizartion consists of people from multiple perspectives, mostly architects and engineers, from vast institutions, all joining as one to advocate for the increase of building more sustainably under the style of what is referred to as green architecture.
What we have done so far
Our organization have caused a surge of favorable opinion about green architecture, make green architecture a possible hypothetical popular design choice for infastructure in the naation. We have opened an open forum for discussion on possible integration and improvement on the architecture landscape, inspiring students from universities with our members as faculty to follow the ideals of the advocacy.
